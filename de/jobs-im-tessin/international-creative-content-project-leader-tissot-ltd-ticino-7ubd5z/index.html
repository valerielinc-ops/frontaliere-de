Hauptaufgaben

  • Your main responsibilities will be as followed:
  • Collaborate and work together with all other HQ marketing departments to create holistic global brand communication campaigns.
  • Develop creative concepts for integrated advertising campaigns (offline and online).
  • Implement content strategy aligned with short-term and long-term marketing targets.
  • Lead the development and adaptation of a large spectrum of content fueling global plans as well as local needs.
  • Supervise and coordinate other sources of content creation (photographers/designers/agencies) by bringing art direction and by reviewing and giving clear guidance for improvement to grant full brand consistency.
  • Oversee video/photo shoots and asset post-production, including final check of all adaptations needed for each channel.
  • Collaborate closely with the graphic design team on creating graphic elements and branding guidelines.
  • Ensure that project management best practices are employed to deliver all international campaigns within strategy, budget and deadline.
  • Spot and seize trends and communication opportunities.
