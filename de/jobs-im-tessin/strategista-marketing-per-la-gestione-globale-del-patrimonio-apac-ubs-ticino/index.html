Rollenüberblick

We are looking for a Marketing Strategist to support the UBS Global Wealth Management (GWM) business across the APAC region.

Working as part of the regional Market Coverage team, you will set a clear, data-informed direction for brand and marketing activation across APAC countries and segments, ensure alignment with divisional priorities, and orchestrate partnerships that amplify growth, reputation, and client impact. Scope & Impact

Work with the GWM regional marketing heads for Greater China, SEA/Japan and Australia and APAC Head of Marketing to shape the end-to-end Brand & Marketing planning process, integrating funnel goals and in-market priorities to deliver measurable outcomes.
