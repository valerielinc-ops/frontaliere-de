Rollenüberblick

Join our global Financial Services and Trade Affairs team and help shape customs and foreign trade processes in an international matrix organization.

As a Global Customs Advisor at our headquarters in Uzwil, you will support both our local business units and our global subsidiaries as a key partner in customs clearance, compliance, and process optimization.

With your expertise, you will drive the design of import and export processes, support the determination of origin of goods, and advise on a wide range of foreign trade matters.

This is an exciting opportunity to take on a role with international reach and real business impact.

Aufgaben

  • You serve as the Global Customs Advisor and provide expert guidance on import/export processes, origin of goods, and other foreign trade-related matters
  • You advise and actively support our business units in Uzwil and global subsidiaries on customs clearance, optimization, and origin-related questions
  • You represent foreign trade topics in internal Group projects, drive process, data, and IT improvements, and support SAP GTS rollouts across Group locations
  • You monitor regional and global foreign trade developments, assess their impact on supply chains and competitiveness, and derive relevant actions
  • You independently manage customs and origin checks, deliver training sessions, and act as the central point of contact for authorities and associations Anforderungen:

  • Service-oriented, resilient, and committed personality with strong communication skills and the ability to interact confidently with internal and external stakeholders in a global environment
