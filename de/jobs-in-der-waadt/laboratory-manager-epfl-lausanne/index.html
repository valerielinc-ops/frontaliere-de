Mission The Laboratory of Photonics and Quantum Measurements is seeking a Laboratory Manager to oversee and implement data management and regulatory compliance and support the laboratory’s experimental research.

The role involves laboratory organisation, equipment maintenance, documentation management, user support and coordination of shared experimental infrastructure.

Unternehmen und Kontext

  • Maintain the safety, organization and cleanliness of laboratories and storage spaces in compliance with laboratory rules and in coordination with group members.
  • Implement a booking system for lab equipment usage by spin-offs.
  • Organise new setups, lab spaces and renovations (Advanced Science Building).
  • Oversee safety audits and lab safety training for internal and external users.
  • Data management and team support: control of laboratory notebooks (TEAMS), management of internal IT simulation software for the laboratory (COMSOL, Lumerical, Ansys and Sonnet) and assist in monitoring and establishing proper documentation of scientific and technical results (via e.g.
  • GIT, Lab/task management software).
  • Initiate weekly laboratory organization meetings with sub-teams.
  • Support the onboarding and training of new students, postdocs, and technical staff in laboratory routines.
  • Cleanroom support (fabrication, packaging, and sample flow coordination): liaise with the R&D team and senior processing engineer to ensure the continuity of fabrication-related activities, including nanofabrication tasks, documentation of established processes and techniques, and equipment requests (platform tools), as well as installation and testing.
  • Contribute to maintaining and improving routines for photonic chip packaging, testing, and experimental integration.
