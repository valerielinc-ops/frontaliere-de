Rollenüberblick

The Director FP&A & Strategic Finance EMEA is a key member of the EMEA Finance Leadership Team, serves as trusted advisor to the Regional Finance Officer and EMEA Leadership Team and acts as a senior leader within the EMEA Finance organization.

The role is responsible for leading the regional Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) and taking a key role in all Strategic Finance activities across EMEA.

The position provides strategic financial leadership to drive profitable growth, optimize resource allocation, improve financial transparency, and strengthen decision-making across all countries within the region.

This is a hands-on individual contributor role, requiring a proactive, can-do mindset and the ability to operate effectively in a lean team environment.

The successful candidate combines deep financial expertise with commercial acumen, leadership capabilities, and a proven ability to influence senior stakeholders in a complex, multinational matrix organization.


MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES & CAPABILITIES

Financial Planning & Analysis

  • Lead the EMEA annual budget, quarterly forecasting, mid-term planning, and monthly performance management reviews.
  • Coordinate planning activities across countries to ensure alignment with regional and global strategic objectives.
  • Develop robust financial models and scenario analyses to support strategic and operational decision-making.
  • Drive accountability for financial and operational performance across the region and monitor against targets, identify risks and opportunities, and recommend corrective actions.
  • Deliver meaningful insights on revenue, margins, operating expenses, profitability, cash flow, and key business drivers.
  • Enhance transparency of financial performance and improve management reporting across the region by providing central reporting, dashboards and advanced analytics.
  • Drive continuous improvement in forecast accuracy, planning methodologies, and financial visibility including representing EMEA in global financial planning projects.

Strategic Finance

  • Serve as trusted advisor to the Regional Finance Officer and EMEA Leadership Team to support profitable growth strategies.
  • Support acquisitions, partnerships, business development initiatives, and strategic investments through financial modeling, fact-based financial analysis and business case development.
  • Support the development of value propositions, pricing strategies, and commercial frameworks that maximize profitability.
  • Oversee analysis of Gross-to-Net deductions, drive pricing, margin management, rebate governance, and commercial investment decisions.
  • Present financial insights and recommendations to executive leadership in a clear and actionable manner

REQUIREMENTS NEEDED

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field; MBA or CFA preferred.
  • Professional accounting/finance qualification (e.g., CPA, ACCA, CIMA) is a plus.
  • Minimum of 10 years of progressive Finance experience including at least 5 years in FP&A leadership roles within a multinational corporate environment.
  • Results focused with the ability to work independently, take ownership and drive initiatives to meet targets and deadlines
  • Position requires excellent analytical, communication and presentations skills along with being a team player with a strong customer focus and a result driven attitude
  • Proven ability to influence and challenge senior stakeholders in a complex, matrixed, multinational organization.
  • Ability to structure, manage, and coordinate complex cross-enterprise projects and processes with multiple stakeholders even in stressful situations
  • Extensive experience with ERP systems (SAP FI/CO, SAP BW), planning platforms, and modern business intelligence tools (Power BI, SAC, or equivalent).
  • Networked and abstract thinking skills, quick comprehension
  • Strong analytical mindset with high financial, data, and digital literacy.
  • Highly effective collaboration skills with a proactive, solution-oriented, and positive mindset.
  • English fluent in spoken and written, any other language an advantage
