Aufgaben

Requirements for employment are a PhD in psychology, excellent scientific achievements appropriate to the career level (publications, third-party funding), the potential to establish an innovative and forward-looking research program in the field of psychological methods research and artificial intelligence, as well as relevant experience in academic teaching.

The successful candidate is expected to be able to teach in German by the end of the tenure track.
